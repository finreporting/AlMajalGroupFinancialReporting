Apr 2026 close
P&L Impact Summary

Total revenue reversal of $324K (K.E. $275K + ENI $49K) flows through April 2026 financials as a one-time reduction. This impacts the comparability of Apr 2026 vs Apr 2025 — adjusted for these reversals, underlying operating revenue runs ~$355K higher than reported $1.03M. Recommend including a footnote in monthly board materials.

Balance Sheet Exposure

Three open BS items aggregating to $4.88M: AMSRE advance ($1.7M asset), Unearned Revenue ($2.5M liability), and SRC retention (~$34K). The AMSRE advance is the highest priority — until vendor AP is received, the asset is unmatched and represents counterparty/timing risk. Net working-capital impact is roughly neutral once AMSRE AP arrives.

SRC Contract — Working Capital Drag

$681K AR with 95% LC-based release pending bank action and 5% retention through June 2027. Embedded gross margin of ~40% on this contract is healthy and consistent with FY 2025 OES portfolio. Monitor bank release timing — any LC delay will push DSO higher and constrain April cash position ($477K).

Accrual Risk — Social Security

The Social Security item is flagged as Action Required because: (1) it spans multiple legal entities (AMTS + JVs), (2) employee transitions create allocation ambiguity, and (3) under-accrual risks a future P&L hit. Schedule Ops review within 5 business days and confirm year-to-date accrual reconciles to payment register.

Recommended Follow-Up Actions

(1) Confirm AMSRE AP receipt timeline with vendor — target Q2 2026 settlement.
(2) Track SRC LC release weekly; escalate to bank relationship manager if > 30 days.
(3) Conclude Social Security accrual scoping call with Husam + Ops before May 2026 close.
(4) Document credit-note approvals in audit file with Ops + H.L. sign-off references.
(5) Establish unearned revenue recognition schedule tied to project milestones.
